Dear Jasmine,

Thank you for starting a Personal Independence Payment (PIP) claim for
James Smith.

For stage 2 of the PIP application, we will ask about their daily living and mobility needs to help us make our decision.

If we need more information, we may invite you to complete an assessment by phone.

When you need to do it by

Please answer all the questions and upload any evidence you have by:
15 October 2024

If you miss this date, we may end this PIP claim. If you need more time to complete, let us know.

What you need to do next

You need to register to access our online service.

Useful tips before you start

It might be helpful to keep a diary for a few days. Note down:

  • how their condition or disability affects their daily life
  • a list of things they need help with or find difficult

You can also gather information to help you fill in the form and it can be uploaded as evidence to support your answers.

How to register for the online service

You will be prompted to register and set a password. We will verify it's you through a text message.

Before you start you will need:

  • the email address we sent this email to and access to your emails
  • your mobile phone
  • James' National Insurance number
  • James' date of birth

Register now

How to complete the form

Once logged in, you will be asked questions about James' health condition or disability and how it affects them. You'll also be prompted to upload any supporting evidence.

You do not need to answer all the questions in one go and can save and return.

Save this email or bookmark the sign in page so you can continue.

Are you receiving Disability Living Allowance (DLA)?

DLA is being replaced by PIP. If you are getting DLA at the moment, this will stop if you do not complete the form by the return date.

Help completing the form

There is information at each step of the online service to help you.

You can also refer to the 'help with answering questions and providing evidence' PDF guide for completing the form. You can download it once you have registered.

If you change your mind and would prefer to complete a paper form instead, contact us so we can post it to you.
