Dear Jasmine,

Thank you for starting a Personal Independence Payment (PIP) claim for
James Smith.

For the second stage of the PIP application, we will ask about their daily living and mobility needs to help us make our decision.

If we need more information, we may invite you to do an assesment by phone.

When to fill in the application by

You will need to answer all the questions and upload any evidence you have by:

31 October 2024

Need more time?

If you need a bit longer to fill in the form, let us know. Otherwise, once the deadline has past, we may need to end the PIP claim.

Our contact details are at the end of this email.


Are they receiving Disability Living Allowance (DLA)?

DLA is being replaced by PIP. If they are getting DLA at the moment, this will stop if you do not complete the form by the return date.


Useful tips before you start

You need to register to access our online service.

We will ask you to enter your email address and some basic information. We will then send a 6 digit code to your mobile phone number. After we have verified it is you, you can set a password.

Before you register, have the following ready:

  • the email address we sent this email to and access to your emails
  • your mobile phone
  • James Smith's National Insurance number

Go to https://www.apply-for-pip.dwp.gov.uk/register


How to complete the form

Once logged in, we will ask you questions about James' health condition or disability and how it affects them. You will also be able to upload any supporting evidence.

You do not need to answer all the questions in one go and can save and return.

Save this email or bookmark the sign in page so you can continue your application.

Help and support

There is information at each step of the online service to help you.

You can download our PDF guide for completing the form once you have registered.

If you change your mind and would prefer to complete a paper form instead, contact us so we can post it to you.
