What to upload



Do upload

  • care or treatment plans
  • test results
  • a repeat prescription list
  • a statement or diary from a carer or family member
  • information from their health or care professionals about their condition or disability and how it affects them

If a document is on paper, you can take a photo or scan it.

Do not upload

  • physical proof of their condition or disability, for example a photo of their prosthetic limb
  • appointment cards or letters
  • general information or fact sheets about their condition or medication

You can add more evidence after you've submitted the application if needed.
