Do you expect difficulties with everyday tasks or getting around to last at least 12 months from when they started?
What we mean by everyday tasks or getting around

By everyday tasks or getting around we mean:

  • preparing food
  • eating and drinking
  • managing your treatments
  • washing and bathing
  • managing toilet needs or incontinence
  • dressing and undressing
  • talking, listening and understanding
  • reading
  • mixing with other people
  • managing your money
  • planning and following journeys
  • moving around
