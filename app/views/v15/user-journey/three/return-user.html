After your PIP application

After you have submitted your PIP application it will be reviewed by DWP. It will also be reviewed by a health professional.

We may need you to attend a consultation with a health professional before we make our decision.

When we will contact you

We will contact you if we need you to attend a consultation. We will also write to you when we have decided whether you can get PIP.

You don't need to contact us unless any of the details you gave us have changed.

You can upload supporting documents

This might be care plans, reports, letters or other information from health or care professionals.
