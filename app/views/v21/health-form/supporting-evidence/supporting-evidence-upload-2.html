Upload a file



Your file must be:

  • a PDF, JPEG, JPG, PNG, DOCX, DOC, or DOT
  • smaller than 10MB

If you have problems uploading your files

If a file is bigger than 10MB, reduce the file size if you can and try again.

If you cannot upload your files you can post photocopies of your documents to DWP instead. Evidence received by post will be considered as part of your PIP claim.

Write your name and National Insurance number on each piece of evidence.
