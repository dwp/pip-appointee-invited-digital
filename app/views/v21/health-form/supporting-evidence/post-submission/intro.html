Supporting evidence

It’s important you provide evidence or information about the impacts of any health conditions or disabilities on your daily life, if you can.

Uploading evidence may mean:

  • we can make a quicker decision on your claim
  • we can make an accurate decision and pay you the right amount
  • you may not need to attend an assessment with a health professional

You should submit your claim on time even if you’re waiting for evidence, for example a hospital or doctor’s letter. You can add more evidence after you've submitted your claim.
