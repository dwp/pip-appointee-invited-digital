What to upload



Do upload

  • care or treatment plans
  • information from your health or care professionals about your condition or disability and how it affects you
  • test results
  • a repeat prescription list
  • a statement or diary from a carer or family member about your condition or disability and how it affects you

If a document is on paper you can take a photo of it or scan it. If a document is digital you can take a screenshot.

Do not upload

  • physical proof of your condition or disability. For example a photo of your prosthetic limb
  • appointment cards or letters
  • general information or fact sheets about your condition or medication

Do not request documents you have to pay for.
