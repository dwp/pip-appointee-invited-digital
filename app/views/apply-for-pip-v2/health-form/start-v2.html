How your health conditions or disabilities affect you

This is stage 2 of your PIP claim. This stage is about your health conditions or disabilities and how they affect you.

We'll ask you questions about your ability to carry out everyday activities. Your answers will help us assess your entitlement to PIP.

Take your time and describe all the help you need. You do not have to answer all the questions in one go. You can save your progress at any point and continue later.

We'll ask you to upload supporting evidence for the claim. Be sure to have this ready or you can log back in to upload.

Before you start

You will need:

  • details of your health conditions or disabilities
  • details of any medicines or treatments you have
  • your health and care professionals' contact details
